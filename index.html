Dobrochannel

Dobrochannel is universal iOS Dobrochan reader.

Known issues: https://github.com/ShadowPrince/dobrochannel/issues

Tips

Content viewer (board & thread):

  • swipe left or right to go to prev/next thread

Attachments viewer:

  • swipe up: info / actions
  • swipe down: close viewer
  • touch: load full image / open link

Post popup viewer

  • swipe left\right: close top popup
  • swipe left\right with 2 fingers: close all popups

New post view

  • swipe right on message field: show preview
  • touch on attachment: delete\change rating
